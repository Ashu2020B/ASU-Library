Assosa University Overview

Established in 2011, Assosa University is a non-profit public higher-education institution located in the large town of Assosa (population range of 10,000-49,999 inhabitants), Benishangul-Gumuz Region. Officially recognized by the Ministry of Education of Ethiopia, Assosa University (ASU) is a coeducational Ethiopian higher education institution. Assosa University (ASU) offers courses and programs leading to officially recognized higher education degrees in several areas of study.
